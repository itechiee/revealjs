Cheese Please!

Introduction:

  • Significant demand exists for high-quality European style cheeses in Malaysia
  • USD 180m in cheese and cheese by-products imported annually
  • Many of these cheeses and other products can be made from buffalo milk

Considerations

Buffalo Milk is used in cheeses, yogurts, drinks, ice creams and other products

Cheese Please!

Why Buffalo Milk:

  • Higher nutrition content – good for consumers
  • Higher fat content (fewer litres needed per kilogram of product) – good for producers
  • Richer, creamier texture – good for cheese and other products
  • More digestible due to protein composition of the milk – good for people with lactose intolerance (which includes much of Asia)

Considerations

Quality Control and Halal Certification

By controlling the raw material supply (operations) and producing the cheese on-site (production) we control the quality of the end product from “seed to plate” which will allow for a superior “farm fresh” product. Use of Halal animal rennet or synthetic rennet will be explored to allow both the production facility and primary dairy products to be certified Halal. These production methods will provide significant differentiation in the marketplace.
